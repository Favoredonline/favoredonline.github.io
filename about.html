Opeyemi T Adeniran

Hi, my name is Opeyemi. I am a Ph.D. student, author, and AI research engineer with specialties in computer science education and artificial intelligence. I am passionate about helping people interested in advancing their careers in information technology.

Founded in July 2018, Favored Online became notably recognized in 2020 following the introduction of "CAREER PATH IN TECH." This initiative has been instrumental in aiding over 1,000 individuals to discover their career trajectories in the IT field.

The success of this initiative has spurred the establishment of Morgan TechFest, a student-led tech conference, that educates students about the influence of technology on their career path.
