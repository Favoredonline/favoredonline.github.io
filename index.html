Welcome to Favored Online Inc.

Initially, Favored Online Skills Acquisition Network, founded in July 2018 and registered in March 2020 under the Nigerian federal government (BN , focuses on providing online IT skills and vocational training. In 2020, the “CAREER PATH IN TECH” initiative was launched to educate people on how to start and grow their careers in tech. Other initiatives that followed include “GROW YOUR CAREER IN TECH” and “BUSINESS PATH IN TECH."

To help address the needs of both aspiring and current tech professionals by integrating our initiatives into a structured framework, we decided to make “Favored Online” a technology holding company, which is now “Favored Online Inc.,” and oversee the administration of the following initiatives:

SHE MODEL TECH:A non-governmental organization whose goal is to empower women in tech through mentorship, online IT skills training, and networking. It aims to build an ecosystem for women to excel and lead.

TechTalents CityA private limited liability company dedicated to fostering a community where learning, skill recognition, and career advancement opportunities seamlessly converge.
